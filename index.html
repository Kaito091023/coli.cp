Unwritten Chapters of Beauty

She was beautiful, but not like those girls in the magazines. She was beautiful, for the
way she thought. She was beautiful, just like the sparkle in her eyes when she talked
about something she loves. She was beautiful, for her ability to make other people smile,
even if she was the complete opposite. No, she wasn't beautiful for something as temporary
as her looks. She was beautiful, not just her body but also deep down to her soul.

Even if loving you felt like a book out in the wind, even if the pages turned too fast for
me to read, I'd still choose you. Even if I didn't get the chance to adore you, to trace your
lines with my fingertip and re-read my favorite parts, I'd still choose you. To live the story
I know we're meant to create together, where each chapter unfolds with new adventures
and joy. I look forward to the many pages yet to be written in our beautiful tale, Tangi.
